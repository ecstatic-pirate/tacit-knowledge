ENTERPRISE ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT PLAYBOOK
======================================
Last Updated: November 2024
Owner: Customer Success Team

OVERVIEW
--------
This document outlines the key processes and best practices for managing enterprise accounts with annual contract values exceeding $100K.

ACCOUNT TIERS
-------------
- Tier 1: $500K+ ACV - Dedicated CSM, quarterly business reviews
- Tier 2: $250K-$500K ACV - Shared CSM, bi-annual reviews
- Tier 3: $100K-$250K ACV - Pooled support, annual reviews

ESCALATION PROCEDURES
---------------------
Level 1: CSM handles directly
- Response time: 4 business hours
- Resolution target: 2 business days

Level 2: CSM + Manager
- Response time: 2 business hours
- Resolution target: 1 business day
- Trigger: Customer expresses dissatisfaction or threatens churn

Level 3: VP involvement
- Response time: 1 business hour
- Trigger: C-suite complaint or contract at risk

KEY RENEWAL MILESTONES
----------------------
- 120 days out: Review usage data and satisfaction scores
- 90 days out: Schedule renewal discussion
- 60 days out: Present renewal proposal
- 30 days out: Finalize terms
- 14 days out: Contract signing target

COMMON CHALLENGES
-----------------
1. Stakeholder changes during contract term
2. Integration issues with customer tech stack
3. Feature requests outside product roadmap
4. Budget constraints at renewal
5. Competition offering lower pricing

SUCCESS METRICS
---------------
- Net Revenue Retention: Target 115%+
- Customer Health Score: Maintain above 75
- Escalation Resolution Rate: 95%+
- QBR Attendance: 90%+ stakeholder participation
